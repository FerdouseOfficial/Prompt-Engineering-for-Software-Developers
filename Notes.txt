Zero Shots Prompting:- 
    In zero shot prompting way you just provide a prompt which answer is already exist you don't need to feed any data for it. You can get the answer from the prompt.

Few Shot Prompting:-
    In few shot prompting way, By only asking or providing a prompt the models will not able to answer that You must feed some information give some context about that concepts the question you are asking or. That is called the few shot prompting.
